4. Why This Is Not Superconductivity
This is not superconductivity.
There are no Cooper pairs, no Meissner effect, no BCS framework.
This is ballistic conduction enabled by structural suppression of scattering.
The physics is based on:
mean free path (λ)
interface roughness
confined-space dynamics
Ramsauer–Townsend minima
field-stabilized gas channels
The behavior resembles zero resistance, but via a different mechanism.

Failure only means fabrication needs adjustment, not that the architecture is wrong.
